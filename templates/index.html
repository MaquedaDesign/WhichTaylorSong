✦ How does this work? (Original study)

This tool is based on a project by astronomer Megan Mansfield (University of Chicago). Each of Taylor Swift's 149 songs was manually scored on 6 emotional dimensions, each using 7 integer levels (−3 → 0 → +3):

Feelings of selfHow good the protagonist feels about themselves
Emotional stageAnger & depression → actively seeking happiness
Relationship seriousnessQuality and depth of the relationship portrayed
Future prospectsOutlook for the relationship's future
Other person's feelingsHow the other person feels about the protagonist
TogethernessHow much time they spend together

The sliders (1–7) map directly onto that same scale, so your answers and the song scores are directly comparable. The algorithm then picks the 5 songs with the smallest Euclidean distance to your position in this 6-dimensional space. The results page shows a 2-D projection so you can see where you land in Taylor Swift's emotional universe.
